## Payoneer — Setup, Fees, and How It Works

Understanding Payoneer's fee structure matters because the costs are hidden in the exchange rate, not listed as explicit line items. Unlike Wise which shows you the exact conversion fee before you confirm, Payoneer applies its rate silently — you see the INR amount that arrives, not the spread they took. After a year of both platforms, I track every withdrawal in a spreadsheet and the 2% rate gap shows up consistently.

Payoneer is the dominant solution for platform-based freelancers. Every major freelancing platform supports Payoneer.

**Setup time:** 20–30 minutes. KYC requires PAN + bank account details. Approval: 2–5 business days.

**Fee structure (March 2026):**

- Receiving from Fiverr/Upwork/platforms: Free (fee absorbed by platform)
- Receiving from another Payoneer account: Free
- Receiving via bank transfer from clients: 1% (capped at $15)

**Withdrawal time to Indian bank:** 2–5 business days typically, occasionally 1 business day.

**Where Payoneer wins:** platform integration (Fiverr, Upwork, Amazon Payments), wider client familiarity, Payoneer-to-Payoneer transfers (free, instant), and the physical Mastercard debit card (useful when travelling internationally).

## Troubleshooting Common Payment Issues

**Wise KYC stuck or rejected**
Most common cause: Aadhaar address doesn't match current address. Use PAN card as primary ID instead. Wise support is responsive — usually resolves within 48 hours.

**Payoneer withdrawal not arriving after 5 days**
Check Payoneer's withdrawal history for error codes. Common issue: bank account details entered incorrectly, IFSC code mismatch. Verify your bank account details in Payoneer settings match exactly what your bank shows.

**Client sending via SWIFT wire to Wise**
Wise charges $4.14 to receive SWIFT wires. Ask clients to send via ACH instead — free on both ends. Give US clients your Wise USD account number and routing number, not the SWIFT/BIC.

**Fiverr "payment failed" when withdrawing to Payoneer**
Usually a name mismatch between your Fiverr account and Payoneer account. Both must match your legal name exactly as on your PAN card.

## FAQ

### Is Wise legal for Indian freelancers?

Yes. Wise is fully RBI-compliant for receiving foreign income. Amounts under $5,000 per transaction don't require a purpose code. Above $5,000, your bank may require a purpose code when funds arrive in your Indian bank account — Wise itself doesn't require it.

### Can I use both Wise and Payoneer simultaneously?

Yes, and you should. There's no restriction on having accounts on both platforms. Most professional Indian freelancers use both — Wise for direct clients, Payoneer for platforms.

### What about PayPal for Indian freelancers?

PayPal's Indian account restrictions are severe — funds must be withdrawn to an Indian bank within 7 days, exchange rates are poor (2–3% below mid-market), and maximum balance is limited. Most Indian freelancers avoid PayPal except when a client specifically requests it.

### Is there a way to receive USD without Wise or Payoneer?

Yes: direct SWIFT wire to your Indian bank account. Your bank gives you a SWIFT code; clients wire money directly. Exchange rate at most Indian banks is 1.5–2% below mid-market — worse than Wise but better than Payoneer. Downside: SWIFT wires cost clients $25–40, making it impractical for smaller invoices.

### How long does Wise take to withdraw to Indian bank accounts?

Same-day for most INR withdrawals initiated before 3pm IST. Occasionally next business day. Rarely takes more than one business day — significantly faster than Payoneer's 2–5 business days.

### Does Payoneer report to Indian income tax authorities?

Payoneer isn't required to directly report to Indian income tax authorities, but your bank will report large inward remittances. You're responsible for declaring foreign income in your ITR regardless of which platform you use. Keep invoice records for every payment received.

Wise transfers to Indian bank accounts via IMPS or NEFT. The fee structure:

Wise uses the mid-market exchange rate with no markup — the same rate you see on Google. The fee is a fixed amount plus a small percentage. At $1,000, you're paying roughly 0.55% total — far below any bank's forex rate.

Transfer time: 1–2 business days to Indian banks via Wise. Some transfers complete same-day.

### Payoneer → Indian Bank Account

Payoneer charges a flat fee per withdrawal:

| Transfer amount | Payoneer fee | Exchange rate markup | Total cost |

## Tax Implications for Indian Freelancers

Receiving foreign payments affects your Indian tax filing. Both platforms interact with Indian tax law differently.

**FEMA compliance:** Payments from foreign clients must comply with FEMA (Foreign Exchange Management Act) regulations. Both Wise and Payoneer are compliant channels for receiving freelance income.

**GST:** If your annual freelance income exceeds Rs 20 lakh, you need GST registration. Exports of services (freelancing for foreign clients) are zero-rated under GST — no GST charged to client, but you can claim input tax credit.

**Income tax:** All freelance income is taxable under "Profits and Gains from Business or Profession" (PGBP). Keep records of every transfer — both platforms provide annual statements.

**Wise advantage for tax:** Wise provides clear transaction statements with INR conversion at the exact rate used. Easy to reconcile with bank statements.

**Payoneer advantage for tax:** Payoneer's annual earnings summary is accepted as supporting documentation by most CA firms handling freelancer tax filings in India.

Neither platform automatically deducts Indian tax — that's your responsibility to file quarterly advance tax if annual liability exceeds Rs 10,000.
