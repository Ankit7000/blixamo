## Why Hetzner Payments Fail From India

There are usually three separate problems:

### 1. RBI 3DS requirements

Most Indian cards expect an OTP-based 3D Secure flow. Many international developer tools do not handle that flow cleanly, so the bank declines the card even if the account has enough balance.

### 2. Recurring billing rules

Getting the card added once is not enough. The first charge may work, then the renewal fails because the bank treats Hetzner as a recurring international merchant.

### 3. International payments disabled by default

Many Indian bank cards ship with international usage blocked or limited. Even when you enable it, the success rate for recurring infra billing is inconsistent.

## Cards That Actually Work Best for Hetzner

### Niyo Global

This is the easiest default recommendation. It behaves more like a forex-friendly international card than a normal Indian debit card, which is why the Hetzner verification charge and later monthly billing are much more likely to work.

### Wise

Wise is the safest option if your infra bill is growing, you pay several international tools from the same account, or you want the least friction across AWS, Hetzner, OpenAI, and similar services.

### HDFC credit card

If you already have a good HDFC credit card, try it before opening another product. Good international credit cards often work better than domestic debit cards for Hetzner.

### Kotak 811 virtual card

Kotak 811 can work as a backup, but I would not pick it as the first recommendation for a server you depend on.

Choose **Niyo Global** if you want the lowest-friction answer for a small Hetzner bill and you like topping up only what you need.

## Common Problems and Fixes

### The card gets declined immediately

Double-check international transactions, app limits, and available balance. If the card is a normal domestic debit card, switch to Niyo or Wise instead of retrying the same bank flow.

### The card was added but the next invoice failed

That is usually the recurring-billing problem. Move Hetzner to Wise or Niyo before the next billing cycle instead of waiting for another failure.

### Hetzner placed a small charge and then reversed it

That is normal for card verification. The real test is whether the card remains attached and your next actual invoice is paid successfully.

## FAQ

### Which card works best for Hetzner from India?

For most solo developers, Niyo Global is the best default answer. Wise is the stronger option if you want one card for multiple international infrastructure bills.

### Does Hetzner accept Indian debit cards?

Some do work, but success is inconsistent. Forex-oriented cards and good international credit cards are much more reliable than standard domestic debit cards.

### Is Wise overkill if I only use Hetzner?

Not necessarily. If Hetzner is your only tool, Niyo is usually enough. If you also pay AWS, OpenAI, Anthropic, or GitHub, Wise becomes much more useful.
